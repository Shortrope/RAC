Donate

The Riverside Art Center, a 501c3 organization, was founded in 2005 by a group of local artists with the support of the Wapakoneta Antique and Specialty Shops, the directors of ArtsPlace, Downtown Partnership, and the Wapakoneta Chamber of Commerce. Within four years, RAC has grown from 6 artists to a membership of over 100 community members, both artists and supporters of the arts. Through the dedication and hard work of our volunteer board members, we have gained a reputation for excellence as a community-minded-organization, a premier fine art gallery, an art education center, and a gathering place for artists of all ages and talents.

From individual to company donations, monetary or tangible, Riverside Art Center accepts and appreciates any form of donation. RAC utilizes these donations for classes, art shows and awards, educational purposes, and other operational costs. We are incorporated and have a Federal Tax ID. Your donation will help us continue to be a positive and healthy influence on our downtown community and the general public. We cannot express enough gratitude to our contributors for the generosity and active participation given by members and non-members alike.

Volunteer

We want to thank our volunteers for being a vital part of our organization. As part of the RAC family, our desire is to help you grow as an important member of our local community. Your willingness to volunteer helps us be good stewards of our donated resources, and it allows us to continue the nonprofit status for the art center.

Our volunteer opportunities include:
Gallery Attendant: the more volunteer Attendants we have, the more times the gallery can be open to the public!
Event Committee: set up tables and refreshments, take photos, greet patrons.

If you'd like to be a RAC volunteer, please fill out the form [below] so we can get a better idea of where you'd best fit in.
