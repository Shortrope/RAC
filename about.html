About Riverside art Center

Riverside Art Center has been home to a variety of art classes and exhibits for all ages in a relaxed and friendly atmosphere since our doors opened in 2006. RAC is dedicated to providing a wide range of programs that are not only entertaining and educational, but also benefit participants through the satisfaction of their personal accomplishments.

We are a not-for-profit community art group founded with the assistance and generosity of the Wapakoneta Chamber of Commerce, Downtown Partnership, the Wapakoneta Antique and Specialty Shops and the directors of ArtsPlace. Our membership has grown to over 100 valued citizens of our community, and we have built a reputation for excellence as a fine art gallery and hub of arts education.

What does RAC have to offer?
Exhibits and Art Shows, Classes, lectures and demonstrations, including programs for children, senior citizens, artists of all skill levels, and artists with disabilities. Poetry readings and improv groups. A venue for local member artists to work, display, and sell their art. We give people a reason to get excited about the arts!
